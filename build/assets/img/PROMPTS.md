# GV Basketball — AI Image Generation Prompts

All images share one **master style** for cohesion. Each prompt below already has the style
prepended. Generated via `codex` CLI → OpenAI Images API. Atmospheric/anonymous only — **no
identifiable faces** (faces obscured, turned, shadowed, or motion-blurred) so no image implies a
specific real coach, athlete, or testimonial.

## Master style (prepended to every prompt)

> Cinematic basketball photography for a premium athletic training brand. Dark, moody, editorial.
> Deep navy (#021F51) and charcoal shadows with dramatic directional gym lighting and a single warm
> orange (#F47B20) accent highlight. High contrast, shallow depth of field, fine film grain,
> professional sports-campaign look. Faces obscured / turned away / in shadow / motion-blurred — no
> identifiable individuals. No text, no logos, no watermarks, no brand marks on apparel.
