About GV Basketball

Founded by Coach Gino Victorino, GV Basketball builds complete basketball players — athletes with elite fundamentals, game IQ, strength, and the character to compete at the highest level.

Who Coach Gino Is

Basketball In His Blood

Coach Gino Victorino is the son of PBA legend Manny Victorino — but he didn't inherit his path. He built his own.

After years of playing at the highest levels and witnessing the gaps in player development across Manila, Gino founded GV Basketball to create a different kind of training — one built on world-class fundamentals, deliberate practice, and genuine character development.

He wanted to bring NBA-level systems to every young athlete across Metro Manila — training in Dasma Makati, Urdaneta Village, and Corinthian Gardens. Not showboating. Not shortcuts. Real development.

How Sessions Work

The Training Method

Every session is designed to maximize growth. We follow a structured, proven framework that separates real training from endless drills.

  • Assessment. We evaluate your current skill level, strengths, and gaps to understand where to focus.
  • Personalized Plan. No two athletes are identical. Your plan is built around your goals and development path.
  • Deliberate Reps. High-quality repetition under coaching pressure — the way the pros train.
  • Accountability. Regular progress reviews, video analysis, and adjusted targets keep you advancing every week.
