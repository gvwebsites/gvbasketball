Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about training at GV Basketball — ages, schedules, programs, costs, and how to get started.

Getting Started

How do I get started with GV Basketball?
Start by sending a request from the "Book a Consultation" page. We'll discuss your athlete's goals, experience, and which program fits best. Your first paid training session then includes a full player evaluation, and from there Coach Gino builds a personalized development plan.
Is the evaluation free?
The consultation is where we talk through goals and the right program fit. The full skills evaluation happens during your first paid training session — not as a separate free assessment. That way the evaluation is real training, with enough time to properly assess your athlete's level and needs.
What ages do you train?
We train athletes from age 3 through professional level. Small Group sessions are for ages 5–20, while Private (1-on-1) and Elite Performance coaching serve all ages — from young beginners to competitive and professional athletes. Every program is tailored to the athlete's level and goals.
Do you train adults?
Yes. Adults train through our Private (1-on-1) and Elite Performance programs, which are open to athletes of all ages — from age 3 through professional level. Whether you're returning to basketball after time away or developing seriously, we'll design a program that fits your schedule and goals. Book a consultation to discuss.

Training & Schedule

What skill levels do you accommodate?
All levels — complete beginners learning to dribble and shoot, intermediate players refining game skills, and competitive players training at an elite level. Every program is personalized. During your consultation we'll assess your athlete's level and recommend the best fit.
What are the training schedules?
Small-group sessions run at set times each week: Dasma, Makati — Mon, Wed & Thu; Urdaneta Village — Fri & Sun; Corinthian Gardens — Sun.
Private 1-on-1 and Elite Performance sessions are scheduled flexibly by appointment, around your availability.
Where are you located?
We train across Metro Manila — Dasma Makati (Mon, Wed & Thu), Urdaneta Village (Fri & Sun), and Corinthian Gardens (Sun) for small groups. Private and Elite Performance sessions are by appointment. We'll confirm the most convenient venue during your consultation.
What if the group times don't fit my schedule?
Private 1-on-1 sessions are flexible and scheduled to fit your family's availability. We'll work with you during the consultation to find times that work.

Programs & Costs

What's the difference between Private, Small Group, and Elite Performance?
Private (1-on-1): fully individualized attention, maximum reps, fastest progression.
Small Group (maximum 6): train with peers at your level in a competitive, game-like environment — skill plus team concepts.
GV Elite Performance: the complete performance system for serious, competitive players — court training, strength & conditioning, recovery, and nutrition. Application required.
Which program should my child join?
During your consultation, Coach Gino will recommend the best fit based on your athlete's level, goals, and learning style. Many beginners thrive in Small Group; athletes needing more attention or chasing specific gaps accelerate with Private; competitive players may benefit from GV Elite Performance.
How much does training cost?
Pricing varies by program and frequency, so we share it during your consultation rather than publishing it online. Book a consultation, tell us your goals and schedule, and we'll give you accurate pricing — no hidden fees.
How are payments handled?
Payments are handled in person at either location. We don't process payments through the website — this keeps things simple and direct for families in Metro Manila.

Logistics & Policies

What should my child bring to training?
Basketball shoes (court shoes for safety and performance), a water bottle, and a ball if they have one. Most importantly: a ready-to-work attitude and willingness to focus.
What's your cancellation and reschedule policy?
Life happens — illness, school events, emergencies. Let Coach Gino know about schedule changes and we'll work with you to reschedule. The specifics are confirmed during your consultation when we set your program and terms.
Can I watch my child train?
Many parents like to stay and observe — it's a great way to see the approach and what your athlete is learning. Ask about observation specifics for your location during the first session.

Coach & Credentials

Who is Coach Gino Victorino?
Gino Victorino is a certified basketball skills trainer and the founder of GV Basketball. His coaching centers on fundamentals, work ethic, basketball IQ, and character — developing confident, skilled, competitive athletes on and off the court.
What are Coach Gino's certifications?
Coach Gino is certified under Phil Handy's 94FEETOFGAME (Phil Handy is an LA Lakers assistant coach and 3× NBA champion) and trained under Micah Lancaster's I'M POSSIBLE methodology (Micah has coached 100+ NBA players). He brings these elite systems to every athlete at GV Basketball.
Can I meet Coach Gino before committing?
Absolutely. Book a Player Consultation and you'll speak with Coach Gino directly about your athlete's goals and the best program fit — a chance to ask questions and get a feel for his coaching approach.
