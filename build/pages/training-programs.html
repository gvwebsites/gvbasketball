Private Training

Who It's For

Exclusive one-on-one coaching for athletes — from ages 3 to professional — pursuing elite-level development through a fully customized performance plan. Every rep, every cue, every session is built around one athlete.

What's Included

  • Player evaluation (movement, skills, mindset)
  • Fully personalized development plan
  • 1-on-1 individualized coaching each session
  • Maximum coach attention and reps
  • Progress tracking and regular feedback

Small Group Training

Who It's For

Athletes ages 5–20 who thrive with peer energy and competition. Groups are capped at a maximum of 6 so every player gets quality reps. All skill levels are welcome — drills are adapted to each player.

What's Included

  • Game-like, competitive drills
  • Maximum of 6 athletes per group
  • Drills adapted to each player's skill level
  • Skill development + team concepts
  • Coach feedback on every player
