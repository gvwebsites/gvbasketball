Private Training

Who It's For

Athletes who need focused, individualized attention. Whether you're a beginner learning fundamentals, an intermediate player refining your game, or an advanced competitor chasing mastery — private training delivers custom development at your pace.

What's Included

  • Player evaluation (movement, skills, mindset)
  • Fully personalized development plan
  • One-on-one skill work each session
  • Maximum coach attention and reps
  • Flexible scheduling around your life
  • Progress tracking and regular feedback

Frequency

Available 1–5 sessions per week. We'll match frequency to your goals during your consultation.

Small Group Training

Who It's For

Players who thrive with peer competition. Small group training brings 4–5 athletes at similar levels together, creating game-like situations where everyone gets quality reps while learning to compete and play as a unit.

What's Included

  • Competitive, game-paced drills
  • Training with athletes at your skill level
  • Individual and team skill development
  • Real-game scenarios and decision-making
  • Coach feedback on every player
  • Structured progression week to week

Frequency

Fixed weekly schedules. Groups typically meet 2–3 times per week. We'll confirm timing during your consultation.

Elite Performance

Who It's For

High school, college, and serious varsity players competing at the highest level. If you're committed to next-level basketball — a competitive team, a scholarship, or pro play — Elite Performance integrates skills, strength, conditioning, and recovery into one comprehensive program.

What's Included

  • Basketball skills training (offensive & defensive)
  • Strength & conditioning workouts
  • Recovery, mobility, and injury prevention
  • Game-film analysis and study
  • Mental performance coaching
  • Structured, periodized progression

Frequency

Intensive: typically 3–5 sessions per week, combining skills, strength, and recovery work. Designed for serious athletes ready to commit.
