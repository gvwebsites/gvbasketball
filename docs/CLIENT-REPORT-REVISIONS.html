01A Fresh, Focused Homepage

We rebuilt the top of the homepage into a calm, confident welcome that gets visitors to the right place quickly.

What Changed

  • Centered welcome: Your message — “Build Better Players. Build Better People.” — now sits front and center with two clear buttons: Book a Consultation and View Training Programs.
  • Simple top menu: Home, About, Programs, Gallery, FAQ and Contact are all one tap away, along with a bright orange booking button that stays visible as visitors scroll.
  • Instagram link: A one-tap Instagram icon now sits in the menu so families can follow along instantly.
  • Coach Gino featured first: Coach Gino appears side by side with his NBA mentors, Phil Handy and Micah Lancaster.
  • Locations on the homepage: Your three training venues now appear right on the homepage, so visitors see where you train without searching.
Live

02A Brand-New Photo Gallery

We created a dedicated gallery so families can see the real GV Basketball experience before they book.

What Changed

  • Real session photos: A clean grid of genuine photos from your actual training sessions and clinics — no placeholder or stock imagery.
  • Easy to find: Gallery was added to both the main menu and the footer.
  • Fast and sharp: The photos are optimized to load quickly and look crisp on phones and large screens alike.
Live

03Real Photos, One Consistent Look

We swapped placeholder imagery for genuine photography across the whole site and gave every photo the same clean, cohesive tone.

What Changed

  • Genuine photography: Real photos of Coach Gino and your athletes now appear across the homepage, programs, about, and gallery.
  • Refreshed About page: Renamed to “About GV Basketball,” with Coach Gino’s portrait, his story, and a photo carousel.
  • One cohesive look: We color-matched every photo to the same clean, cool tone, so the whole site feels like one polished brand rather than a mix of sources.
Live

04Clearer Training Programs

We made it simple to compare programs at a glance and understand how booking works — without listing prices online.

What Changed

  • Three clear programs: Private Training, Small Group (up to 6 athletes, all skill levels welcome), and Elite Performance (which now includes aqua training).
  • Updated photos: The program images were refreshed with real session photography.
  • Simple schedule: A clean schedule shows which days each venue runs — Dasma Makati (Mon, Wed, Thu), Urdaneta Village (Fri, Sun), and Corinthian Gardens (Sun).
  • How booking works: A short, friendly step-by-step explainer sets expectations for booking and payment, which is handled privately rather than shown online.
  • Personal pricing: Pricing stays customized and is shared during the consultation.
Live

05One-Tap Consultation Booking

Booking a consultation is now effortless from anywhere on the site — every button opens the same quick form right where the visitor is.

What Changed

  • Opens anywhere: Every “Book a Consultation” button now opens the same quick pop-up form — no page jumps and no dead ends.
  • Location & day picker: Parents choose a preferred venue, and the available days update automatically for that location.
  • Only the essentials required: Phone or Instagram and preferred time are optional, so the form stays short and friendly.
  • Spam protection: A quiet security check keeps junk submissions out of your inbox.
  • Confirmation emails: Requests go straight to your coaching inbox, and both you and the parent receive an email that includes the chosen location and day.
  • No more dead links: Older buttons that used to send people to a separate page now open this form directly.
Live

06Premium Polish

A set of finishing touches that make the whole site feel more premium and consistent.

What Changed

  • Premium gold accents: A tasteful gold accent was added to key call-to-action areas to complement the GV navy and orange.
  • Consistent buttons: Buttons now keep clean white text when you hover over them, fixing an earlier color glitch.
  • Cleaner footer: The footer was rebuilt to be simple and elegant, using your official GV wordmark logo.
  • Newsletter tidied away: We removed the newsletter sign-up strip for now, since it isn’t being used yet — it can be switched back on later without rebuilding it.
Live

07A Simpler Contact Page

With your venues now shown on the homepage, the Contact page is focused purely on helping people reach you.

What Changed

  • Focused on reaching you: The page now leads with your Instagram and email, plus a simple message form.
  • Venues moved to the homepage: The list of training locations was moved to the homepage where it’s seen sooner.
Quality Check

Tested & Live

After making these changes, we checked the live website to make sure everything works as intended.

What We Verified

  • The new menu, Instagram link, and gallery all work on every page.
  • The consultation pop-up opens from every “Book a Consultation” button, and location/day selection behaves correctly.
  • Confirmation emails reach both your coaching inbox and the parent.
  • “Book a Consultation” wording is consistent across the whole site.

Always Up to Date

We cleared the website’s caches after each update, so visitors always see the newest version right away — no waiting and no stale pages.
