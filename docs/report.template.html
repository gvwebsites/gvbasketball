05Where your booking requests go

Every booking and every message from the website is sent to your business address and then forwarded to your personal inbox — so you never have to “check the website.”

What this means for you day-to-day

  • You receive every request by email. Booking requests and contact messages are sent to info@gvbasketball.com, which automatically forwards to your personal Yahoo (ymail.com) inbox. Open your normal email and it's there.
  • Booking requests are also saved on the website. They don't just live in email — they're stored in your booking system so nothing gets lost.

How to view or edit a booking yourself

  • Go to gvbasketball.com/wp-admin and log in with your admin account.
  • Open LatePoint → Bookings in the left menu.
  • From there you can view, reschedule, or cancel any booking, and see the client's contact details.
  • Times are shown in Manila time; amounts are in Philippine Peso (₱).
By design

06Payments — handled by you, not the website

The website does not collect payments. This was intentional: pricing stays private and payment is arranged with you directly, in person or however you prefer.

If you ever want to accept payments online

It wasn't part of this build, but the booking system can support online payments later. A web developer can connect a payment provider (for example PayMongo, GCash, or a card gateway) and switch it on. Just say the word and we can scope it as a separate, optional add-on — nothing about the current site needs to change for it.
