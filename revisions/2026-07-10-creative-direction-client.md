## Part A — Creative Direction (Brand Evolution)

Client's framing: *"We're no longer building only a basketball training business. We're building an institution. The website should begin reflecting that vision."*

### 1. Less Information. More Emotion.
- Replace long explanations with visuals + headlines.
- Example: instead of "STEP 4 – Weekly Skill Development" + 120 words, use:
  - **WEEKLY SKILL DEVELOPMENT** — "Every rep has a purpose."
  - Large image + three icons: ✓ Shooting ✓ Decision Making ✓ Confidence
- "People remember headlines, not paragraphs."

### 2. Replace Stock Images
- Remove generic/stock basketball photos; use GV's own photography.
- Goal: visitors feel "This is GV," not "any basketball website."
- Client will invest in professional photography/video for the site.
- **Client will send:** certifications + more authentic photos.

### 3. Make Coach Gino More Visible
- Founder should feel present across the experience: teaching, demonstrating, encouraging athletes, speaking with parents, celebrating progress, coaching on court, leading discussions.

### 4. Build More Emotion
- Visitors should *feel* something before they *learn* something.
- Suggested opening scenes: father tying son's shoes, Coach Gino teaching, a player missing then improving, a parent smiling after a game, team huddles, quiet coach–athlete moments.
- Less explanation, more storytelling.

### 5. Showcase Results
Feature prominently:
- 100+ student-athletes mentored
- Schools represented
- Parent testimonials
- Player success stories
- Team accomplishments
- Before-and-after development
- Community impact

### 6. Introduce GV Elite Academy
- GV Basketball stays as the coaching brand.
- Begin creating a **premium identity for GV Elite Academy** — eventually its own website + visual identity.
- Reference brands: IMG Academy, Apple, Nike, Porsche, Rolex. Luxury. Minimal. Intentional.

### 7. Future Website Structure (long-term)
- **HOME** — one powerful video, one statement, one CTA.
- **THE FOUNDER** — the story, the mission, the reason GV exists.
- **THE METHOD** — simple, visual, minimal copy.
- **RESULTS** — players, parents, schools, success stories.
- **GV ELITE ACADEMY** (separate tab, different offer/branding) — residential academy, leadership, character, recovery, film study, dorm life.
- **FOUNDING PARTNERS** — dedicated page for corporate partners (Ayala Land, HSBC, Nike, Mercedes-Benz, Century Tuna, etc.).
- **APPLY** — premium application experience.

### Long-Term Goal
The next version should make parents, athletes, and corporate partners feel they're looking at **the beginning of a lasting institution** — not just another basketball training business.
