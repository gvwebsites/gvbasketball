## 3. Brand Separation Recommendation (client's)
- **GV Basketball** = current revenue business (private, small group, elite performance).
- **GV Elite Academy** = institutional vision; inspires parents, attracts sponsors, builds anticipation.
- Two distinct brands on the same website for now; Academy may become a standalone site/domain later.

## 4. Overall Design Direction (site-wide)
- Feel: Luxury · Minimal · Emotional · Cinematic · Premium · Confident. Reference brands: Nike, Apple, Rolex, Porsche, IMG Academy.
- Not information-heavy, not text-heavy. Photography and video tell the story.
- **Less text, more impact:** large headlines, one supporting sentence, powerful photography, icons. "Every section should communicate one message, not five."
- **Use real photos:** replace as much stock as possible with authentic GV photography/video.
- **Show Coach Gino more:** teaching, correcting, encouraging, speaking with parents, celebrating, film sessions, whiteboard sessions, high-fives, huddles.
- Generous spacing; every page intentional.
- A concept image was referenced as creative direction (*"don't copy the attached image exactly — use it as a creative reference"*). **Not yet received — request from client.**
